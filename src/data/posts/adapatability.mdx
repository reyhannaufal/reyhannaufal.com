## The challenge

Being an adult is a challenge, there are many things that you need to learn to overcome it.

> But how to do it? How to adapt to the challenge? How to overcome it?`

### Ways of thinking

The way of we think our mindset is a powerful technique that can help us to overcome the challenges. The minset is
a powerful technique that can help you to preceive the problem that you are facing in different viewpoints. The mindset
is the source of our ability to adapt to the challenge. Our mindset drives our, action which eventually drives our growth

### Growth Mindset

> In a growth mindset, people believe that their most basic abilities can be developed through dedication and hard work—brains and talent are just the starting point. This view creates a love of learning and a resilience that is essential for great accomplishment.

This is how growth mindset view these things

- `Desires - I want to learn new things. I am eager to take risks.`
- `Skills - Is this really my best work? What else can I improve?`
- `Effort - I know this will help me Even though it is difficult`
- `Motivation - I am motivated to learn.`
- `Setbacks - I’ll use another strategy, my mistakes help me learn.`
